My Soft Skills

Time Management

Time management refers to the process of organizing and planning how to allocate time effectively to accomplish specific tasks, projects, or goals.

Problem Solving

Problem-solving refers to the process of finding solutions to complex or challenging issues encountered in various contexts, whether personal, professional, or academic.

Negotiation

Negotiation is a dialogue or discussion between two or more parties with the aim of reaching a mutually beneficial agreement or resolving a conflict.

Adaptability

Adaptability refers to the ability to adjust or change in response to new conditions, situations, environments, or challenges.

Motivation

Motivation refers to the inner driving force or the reason behind an individual's actions, desires, and behaviors

Pressure Handling

Handling self-pressure refers to managing and coping with the stress, expectations, and demands one places on oneself. It involves maintaining a healthy balance between setting high standards for oneself.
