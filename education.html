My Education

Primary School

I started my school life studied at SK Taman Desa in 2010-2012.Studying at Sekolah Kebangsaan Taman Desa was an enriching and memorable experience. Nestled within the vibrant community of Taman Desa, the school provided a conducive environment for learning and personal growth. The school's dedicated educators fostered a nurturing atmosphere, encouraging students to excel not only academically but also in various co-curricular activities. The diverse range of subjects offered equipped students with a well-rounded education, emphasizing both theoretical knowledge and practical skills. One of the remarkable aspects was the emphasis on holistic development. Beyond academics, the school encouraged participation in sports, arts, and extracurricular clubs.

Primary School

Moving from Sekolah Kebangsaan Taman Desa to SK Bandar Tasik Puteri marked a significant chapter in my educational journey. The transition brought with it new challenges, opportunities, and a fresh environment that contributed to my growth and learning. SK Bandar Tasik Puteri provided a welcoming atmosphere, easing the adjustment period and making me feel part of the school community from the beginning. The supportive teachers and staff helped me acclimate to the new setting, ensuring a smooth transition academically and socially. The transition to SK Bandar Tasik Puteri brought about personal growth, resilience, and adaptability. It taught me the importance of embracing change, stepping out of my comfort zone, and seizing opportunities for growth. In retrospect, my experience at SK Bandar Tasik Puteri was transformative. The school provided a platform to excel academically, develop social skills, and shape my character, leaving a lasting impact on my educational journey.

Secondary School

Studying at SMK Bandar Tasik Puteri was a transformative phase in my educational journey in 2016-2020. The transition from primary to secondary school marked a significant milestone, bringing new challenges, opportunities, and personal growth. The school's vibrant atmosphere and strong sense of community provided a conducive environment for learning and self-development. The dedicated educators at SMK Bandar Tasik Puteri went beyond imparting academic knowledge; they served as mentors, guiding and inspiring us to reach our full potential. The curriculum at the school was comprehensive, offering a wide array of subjects and co-curricular activities. This diversity allowed students to explore various fields of study, nurturing both academic excellence and practical skills. The supportive and inclusive environment encouraged collaboration among peers. Forming friendships and bonds with classmates and teachers created a sense of belonging and unity within the school community. SMK Bandar Tasik Puteri provided ample opportunities for personal growth and self-discovery. Over the years, I gained not only knowledge but also invaluable life lessons, resilience, and the determination to overcome challenges. Reflecting on my time at SMK Bandar Tasik Puteri, I am grateful for the enriching experiences, the guidance of my teachers, and the friendships forged. The school played a pivotal role in shaping my academic excellence, character development, and preparing me for the journey ahead.

University

At UiTM, the emphasis on academic excellence was apparent in its rigorous curriculum and esteemed faculty members. The lecturers' dedication and expertise in their respective fields not only imparted knowledge but also inspired critical thinking and intellectual curiosity among students. The university's multifaceted approach to education extended beyond textbooks. Engaging in practical projects, research initiatives, and industry collaborations provided hands-on experience and a glimpse into the real-world applications of our studies. Beyond academics, UiTM offered a rich tapestry of extracurricular activities and clubs catering to varied interests. Whether participating in sports, cultural events, or academic clubs, these activities fostered a sense of camaraderie, leadership, and personal development. One of the most valuable aspects of UiTM was its emphasis on holistic growth. The university encouraged students to embrace diversity, instilled values of integrity and professionalism, and nurtured a strong sense of community. Moreover, UiTM served as a platform for networking and building connections. Engaging with alumni, industry professionals, and guest speakers through seminars and workshops broadened horizons and provided valuable insights into future career paths. Reflecting on my time at UiTM, I am grateful for the invaluable experiences, friendships forged, and the knowledge gained. The university not only prepared me academically but also equipped me with the skills, confidence, and resilience needed to navigate the complexities of the professional world.
